About

The app's intended purpose is to make nutritional information for the three main macronutrients open and accessible by all. For each item in our database, you'll find its fat, carbohydrates and protein count (in grams). Using the macronutrients and serving size, we calculate the calorie count based on the following equation:

Fat(g) x 9

Carbohydrates(g) x 4

Protein(g) x 4

The serving size is then multipled by the sum of each macronutrient.

To contribute an item, simple click the 'create' link and enter all the required fields. If you wish to edit, click the 'edit' link.

Enjoy!
